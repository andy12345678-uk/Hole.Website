Two Clubhouses

The two clubhouses provide a luxurious and welcoming environment for golfers to relax and socialize. Designed with elegance and functionality in mind, they offer a range of amenities, including comfortable lounge areas, private locker facilities, and event spaces for meetings or celebrations. The clubhouses also house dining areas and bars, where players can enjoy a hearty meal or a refreshing drink while reflecting on their game.. .

Hotel Accommodation

For those looking to extend their visit, the on-site hotel offers a seamless blend of comfort and convenience. The accommodations range from cozy standard rooms to luxurious suites, catering to solo travelers, families, and corporate groups. Guests can enjoy modern amenities and impeccable service, ensuring a relaxing stay. The hotel’s prime location within the complex allows easy access to the golf courses and other facilities, making it ideal for golf vacations, weekend getaways, or business retreats.

Restaurants and Cafe

Dining options at the facility are designed to suit all tastes and occasions. The restaurants serve an array of delicious dishes, from local specialties to international cuisine, prepared by skilled chefs using fresh ingredients. Whether you're enjoying a celebratory dinner or a casual lunch, the ambiance and service are impeccable. For lighter fare, the café offers a range of snacks, pastries, and beverages, perfect for a quick bite or a relaxed coffee break. Together, these options ensure that visitors are well-fed and rejuvenated throughout their stay.

Pro Shop

The pro shop is a golfer’s paradise, offering a comprehensive selection of equipment, apparel, and accessories. Stocked with top brands, it provides everything from high-performance clubs and golf balls to stylish and functional clothing. The knowledgeable staff are always on hand to assist with expert advice, helping players select the best gear to suit their needs and improve their game. Whether you’re a beginner looking for basic equipment or an experienced player seeking the latest innovations, the pro shop ensures you’re fully equipped for the course.
